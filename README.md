Are you tired of losing your keys right when you're about to step out ? This might be the solution for you !

The application starts a camera feed and, when it detects movement (histogram comparison) sends a screenshot to Gemini. Gemini tells us which objects in the list (Keys, Phones, etc) are in the 
picture and give us a bonding box.
The latest pictures of each item are stored in a DB with a timestamp and can be pulled up from your phone app to know when and where your keys were last seen.
